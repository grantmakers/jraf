Our Focus

Founded in Nov. 2007, the J.R. Albert Foundation provides support to nonprofit organizations in Illinois, Wisconsin, Michigan, Missouri and Kansas whose purpose is:

  • To increase access to healthy foods by developing local food delivery systems, increasing distribution of fresh foods, and work in sustainable agriculture, urban farms, and community gardens.
  • To provide food to those who are in need, by supporting large regional food banks.
  • To provide nutrition and wellness education and opportunities, particularly in economically disadvantaged populations. We are especially interested in collaborative efforts towards community change.
